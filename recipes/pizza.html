pizza recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular and versatile dish originating from Italy, consisting of a round, flatbread crust topped with various ingredients. Typically, these toppings include tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and various additional items such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. The pizza is then baked in an oven, resulting in a delicious and satisfying meal enjoyed worldwide. Pizza comes in many variations, ranging from traditional Margherita to creative combinations that cater to diverse tastes and preferences.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center.
    3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the well. Stir until a dough forms.
    4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours or until doubled in size.

  3. Roll Out the Dough:
    1. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two if making two pizzas.
    2. Roll out each portion into a thin round on a floured surface.
  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    1. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Finish and Serve:
    1. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
