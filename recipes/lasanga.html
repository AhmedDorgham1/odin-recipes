Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish composed of layers of wide flat pasta sheets, rich and savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. The layers are stacked and baked until the pasta is tender, and the cheese on top is golden and bubbly. This hearty and flavorful dish is a popular comfort food, known for its satisfying combination of textures and tastes.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
  • 1 cup (about 4 ounces) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups (about 8 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Ground Beef: In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add Onion and Garlic: Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in Marinara Sauce: Pour the marinara sauce into the skillet. Stir well and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in a baking dish.
    2. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    3. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    4. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of cheese.
  6. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let it Rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve and enjoy your quick and delicious lasagna!
